Automatic Coil gun

Fire!

Designer

XuYunxiu, LuoShan, ZhaoMi

Date

2019.8.16

Category

Control

This coilgun was asked to be made in limited time which less than 4 days for electronic design competition. To realize the function specified, we used servo motor-based 2 dof cradle head and OpenMv to finish our task.

The coil was winded by hand and we use a booster circuit to increase the drive voltage up to 200V. The Conventional relay cannot endure the current of this system, hence, we used a servo motor to control 2 metal plate's on and off to realize the function of relay and can endure high current. We control the electrification time to control the shooting distance.
