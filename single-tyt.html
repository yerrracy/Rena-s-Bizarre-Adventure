"Jumper" is a wechat tiny game which has become fashionable for a time. The only operation is to press your smartphone at any place and control the time you press down. Then you can make the tiny character jump over the platform. Once you fall off, the game will over.

We came out our algorithm on OpenCV python and use a series of approaches to find the origin and the terminal, then we can calculate the distance and convert the distance to the time you will press. We use the capacitive touch pen to press down, and build our machine with 3D printing. And it can achieive a relatively high score than most of the human.
